Quality Policy

Tony’s Tuna International Pty Ltd is committed to comply with requirements and to continually improve the effectiveness of its management system to ensure that:

It provides a framework for establishing and reviewing quality objectives.

•  Is communicated within the organisation.

•  Regular audits and management reviews are conducted to monitor the effectiveness and suitability of the system.

•  All current and relevant legislation and regulations are complied with or exceeded and where no appropriate regulations exist, appropriate standards are adopted.

•  An effective mechanism exists for setting and reviewing quality objectives and targets.

•  Documented procedures are established and maintained to detail how quality matters are communicated to all employees, customers and contractors, and how relevant information is made available to the public.

•  Working with our clients to ensure we have identified their needs and are supplying them.

•  All aspects of Tony’s Tuna International Pty Ltd business are run in such a manner so as to minimise and handle any incidents and emergencies should they occur.
