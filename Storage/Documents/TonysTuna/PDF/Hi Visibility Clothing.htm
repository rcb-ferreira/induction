Hi Visibility Clothing

 

In order to protect our workers whilst working for Tony’s Tuna International and to comply with WHS legislation, the wearing of appropriate High Visibility clothing is recommended when working in traffic zones.

Hi Visibility Clothing is available for Day Use and for Night Use (with reflective tape fitted). Workers should select the appropriate item for the conditions they will be working in. If unsure, the Night Use High Visibility clothing should be selected. Night rated High Visibility clothing may also be used for darker indoor areas or for workers who are in areas where vehicles move from outdoors to indoors.

Traffic zones (excepting designated walkways and footpaths) are as follows:

•  Front Loading Apron

•  Bait Store

•  Superfreeze

•  Plate Freeze

•  ULT Container Loading Dock

•  Rear Loading Apron

•  Farm Shed Work Apron

Net Shed work area is not a traffic zone at most times. High Visibility Clothing should be worn during any crane, forklift, truck and tractor operations in the Net Shed area.

Hi Visibility Clothing should be worn when traversing across these areas outside of designated walkways.
